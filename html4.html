Top 5 Career Options After 12th Arts

1. Journalism & Mass Communication

A dynamic field for those interested in storytelling, media, broadcasting, and news. Can lead to careers in print, digital, and broadcast media.

2. Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA)

An excellent choice for students with a creative flair, focusing on visual arts, sculpture, painting, and design.

3. Psychology

A fascinating field that studies human behavior and the mind. Opens opportunities in counseling, therapy, and research.

4. Fashion Designing

An exciting field for students with an interest in fashion, textiles, and clothing design, offering a variety of career opportunities in the fashion industry.

5. Event Management

An ideal career for students who enjoy planning and organizing. This field covers everything from corporate events to weddings and entertainment.
