Top 5 Career Options After 12th Science (Biology) in India

1. MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)

The most preferred medical field for students aiming to become doctors. Requires NEET qualification.

2. BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery)

An excellent career choice for those interested in dentistry and oral healthcare.

3. BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery)

A blend of modern medicine and traditional Ayurveda, widely practiced in India.

4. BHMS (Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery)

A promising field in homeopathic medicine, focusing on holistic treatment.

5. BSc Nursing

A rewarding career in healthcare, essential for patient care and hospital management.
