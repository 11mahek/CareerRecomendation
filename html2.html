Top 5 Career Options After 12th Science (Mathematics)

1. Engineering (B.Tech/B.E.)

The most popular career choice for Mathematics students. Offers various branches like Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, and Electrical Engineering.

2. BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

A great option for students interested in computer science and programming. This can be followed by a Master's in Computer Applications (MCA).

3. Mathematics Teaching

A rewarding career option for those passionate about teaching and sharing their love for mathematics. Opportunities exist in schools, colleges, and coaching institutes.

4. Data Science & Analytics

A rapidly growing field focused on interpreting and analyzing complex data, suitable for students who are good with numbers and statistics.

5. Architecture

A creative and technical field that involves designing and planning buildings and structures. Ideal for students with a keen interest in design and engineering.
