Top 5 Career Options After 12th Commerce

1. Chartered Accountancy (CA)

A highly respected and rewarding career choice in accounting, auditing, and taxation. Requires passing the CA exams and a training period.

2. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

A popular undergraduate program for those interested in management, business operations, and entrepreneurship. Often followed by an MBA.

3. Company Secretary (CS)

A career focusing on corporate law, governance, and compliance. CS professionals handle legal matters in companies and corporations.

4. Hotel Management

A dynamic and growing industry, suitable for students who are interested in hospitality, tourism, and customer service management.

5. Digital Marketing

An exciting and fast-growing field focusing on online marketing strategies, social media, and e-commerce. Ideal for creative and analytical individuals.
